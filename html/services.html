If you're referring to flight services, here are some facilities that you could consider adding to your services: In-flight entertainment: You could offer a range of entertainment options, such as movies, TV shows, and games, to keep passengers entertained during their flight.



  • Wi-Fi: Offering Wi-Fi onboard flights is becoming increasingly popular, as passengers want to stay connected to their work or social networks.
  • Power outlets and USB ports: Providing power outlets and USB ports at each seat allows passengers to charge their electronic devices during the flight.
  • Extra legroom: You could offer seats with extra legroom for passengers who require more space, such as tall individuals or those with disabilities.
  • In-flight meals: Offering meals or snacks on board can make the flight experience more enjoyable for passengers. You could also consider providing special meals for passengers with dietary restrictions.
  • Priority boarding: You could offer priority boarding to passengers who require special assistance or who have paid for a higher fare class.
  • Lounge access: You could offer lounge access to passengers flying in a higher fare class or who hold loyalty program status. The lounge can provide a comfortable space to relax before or after a flight, along with complimentary drinks and snacks.
  • Baggage tracking: Providing passengers with real-time updates on the location of their luggage can ease their travel concerns and help prevent lost or delayed baggage.
  • Mobile check-in: Offering mobile check-in options can streamline the check-in process for passengers and reduce wait times at the airport.
  • Personalized services: You could offer personalized services, such as a dedicated concierge or personalized travel recommendations, to help passengers make the most of their travel experience.
